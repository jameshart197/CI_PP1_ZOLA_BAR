Share Drinks

At Zola our inspiration and ethos is based on the very simple concept of sharing. When people share drink, or indeed anything else, it creates a bond. It inspires conversation, interaction and creates an amtmosphere that is at once both comfortable and sociable. We were inspired by Spanish Tapas and the unforgettable feeling of going out with a group of friends and passing food across the table. Going out for a drink is already an important social standard in England, and we wanted to take that a step further and introduce sharing into that space to create the perfect social atmosphere for creating memories.
